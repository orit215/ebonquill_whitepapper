# Technical Aspects

### Powered by Unreal Engine

Ebonquill utilizes Unreal Engine 5.3.4 to create a visually rich and dynamic experience. With Nanite, the game boasts detailed, high-quality environments that load with incredible efficiency. Virtual Shadow Maps ensure sharp, accurate lighting, while Niagara enhances gameplay with smooth, realistic particle effects. Real-time global illumination and reflections through Lumen provide a natural and immersive lighting system that adapts to the changing game world. These technologies work seamlessly together to deliver an engaging and polished experience for players.

### Platforms and Compatibility

The game is designed with accessibility and flexibility in mind. Players can enjoy the game across multiple platforms, ensuring a unified and seamless experience regardless of the device. Whether you're at home on a PC or on the go with mobile devices, you can continue your adventure with no barriers.

* **Cross-Platform Availability:** Ebonquill will launch on **PC**, with plans for future releases on **Android, Mac, and iOS**.
* **Seamless Experience Across Devices:** Whether you're playing on a high-performance PC or mobile, the gameplay remains consistent and optimized for each platform.
* **Cross-Play Enabled:** Players can connect and compete with others across different devices, whether itâ€™s in PvP, dungeons, or social features like guilds.
* **Unified Community:** No matter the platform, players can join the same community, ensuring a global and connected player base.
